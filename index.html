About Me

Hello, thanks for your interest in my portfolio! I'm Aurelio Trigueros Miravalls, but everyone calls me Aure. I got my degree in Game Design and Development at the Universitat Jaume I. I've always thought of videogames as an art that I wanted to be part of, since I played Pokémon Diamond on my DS when I was a kid.

As a student of a very interdisciplinary career, I have been able to excel in programming and design, although I have basic notions of everything that is needed in a video game. I have developed for many platforms with different engines and languages, so I adapt well to the proposed situations.

People usually laugh a lot with me. I like weightlifting, medieval fantasy and birds.

Projects

-Guardianes de Segorbe- 2024

Augmented reality cultural game for mobile devices set in the town of Segorbe, Castellón. Developed with Unreal Engine 5, it uses Google's AR Core library as well as Location Services to use the phone's geolocation. I did this project working for the Instituto de Nuevas Tecnologías de la Imagen of the UJI.

I was the lead programmer along with another person in the project. It has been well received and has been featured in several news. We also presented an article about it in Congreso Español del Videojuego (CEV).

-Voodoo Wars!-
2023

Voodoo Wars! is the biggest game I've made so far. It's my final degree project and it's a 3D fighting game with both online and local multiplayer. It was made with Unity.

It has a matchmaking system to match two players who are looking game at the same time anywhere. For this, I used Unity Services to host the server.

I did everything but the arena model, including a custom illumination shader, the character models and the UI.

-Typeforce-
2021

Game made with Phaser in JavaScript, made for a web games subject. It consists of typing the words that appear on top of the enemies to kill them, using the keyboard. This makes you improve your mechanography skills. The enemies come at you in waves, increasing the difficulty after each wave.

It was voted best game of the year of the subject by people's vote.

-Final Duel-
2021

Mobile game made with Android Studio in Kotlyn. It consists of a game similar to Osu in which you have to press the circles just when the arc is closing to attack your opponent. If you hit it too early or it runs out of time, your opponent hits you.

When you defeat the opponent, a bong appears and you have to press it as fast as possible to increase your score.
