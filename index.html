Hello and welcome to my portfolio!

My name is Aurelio Trigueros Miravalls, but you can call me Aure.
I am a passionate Shader & VFX Technical Artist currently living in Benidorm, Alicante.

I am proud of being a self-motivated and adaptative learner, driven by enthusiasm for game creation. I believe in bringing creativity and technical skills to every project I undertake.

My journey has equipped me with a deep understanding of the game development pipeline, allowing me to collaborate effectively with designers and artists.

People usually laugh a lot with me. I really like medieval fantasy and every class of bird. I enjoy weightlifting too!
