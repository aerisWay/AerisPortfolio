About Me

Hello, thanks for your interest in my portfolio! I'm Aurelio Trigueros Miravalls, but everyone calls me Aure. I graduated in Game Design and Development at the Universitat Jaume I. I've always thought of videogames as an art that I wanted to be part of, since I played Pokemon Diamond on my DS when I was a kid.

As a student of a very interdisciplinary career, I have been able to excel in programming and design, although I have basic notions of everything that is needed in a video game. I have developed for many platforms with different engines and languages, so I feel that I adapt well to the proposed situations.

People usually laugh a lot with me, I like weightlifting, medieval fantasy and birds.

Projects

-Guardianes de Segorbe- 2024

Augmented reality cultural game for mobile device set in the town of Segorbe, Castellón. Developed with Unreal Engine 5, it uses Google's AR Core library as well as Location Services to use the phone's geolocation. I did this project working for the Instituto de Nuevas Tecnologías de la Imagen of the UJI.

I was the lead programmer along with another person in the project, creating the core for the levels and some of them. It has been well received and has been featured in several news, and we also presented an article in Congreso Español del Videojuego (CEV) about the project, of which I am the first author.
